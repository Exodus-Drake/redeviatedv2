Topic: Old Deviantart user account deactivations

It's really sad going through the wayback machine and finding all artist accounts then going to their modern page and seeing it's just gone... all the lost art, all the lost memories, and it's not even just because of the Eclipse and WIX acquisition, just users leaving, weather it be one reason or another (accounts pre-2014 to some of the first days dA opened it's doors...).

It's heart breaking, not being able to see the art they made, which is now locked away in dA's storage never to be seen again (I mean, really dA stores it all, especially now with deleted comments being quote unquote"hidden". Damn dataset pricks...).

It hurts to see people you like gone, especially seeing this art in the the wayback machine where all you see is the thumbnail, but the page itself is just gone, just leaving that tiny frame of something that looks nice but you can't view it in full and appreciate it...

Ofc that being said, the artist that left had all the rights to deactive and leave, it's not really wrong for them to have done, but to just leave and basically wipe away their existance completely is just saddening for me personally.

Sometimes I wish I could find those artist again and just ask them if they are alright, just as a form of closure, to know they are at least doing well now. It's sad to see an artist just lose their passion for art, music, writing, crafts, etc etc... but if they are alright and still alive, then that's great to hear...

I wonder how often or if they ever just comeback, just to check on the site... Wonder how disappointed they are seeing how things have gone, not just for Deviantart, but for the internet in general...
